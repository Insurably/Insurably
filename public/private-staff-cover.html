What is Private Staff Cover?

Private Staff Cover is specialist insurance for high net worth individuals who employ domestic staff within their homes or estates. It forms an essential part of a comprehensive high value household insurance policy, offering legal liability protection, accident cover, and support for employment disputes.

Whether you employ a nanny, housekeeper, gardener, chef, or chauffeur, Private Staff Insurance helps safeguard both you and your employees—ensuring you're covered for workplace incidents, injuries, or allegations.

Who Needs Private Staff Liability Insurance?

This cover is vital for individuals who employ one or more staff members in a private domestic setting. You may need Private Staff Liability Insurance if you have:

  • Full-time or part-time nannies – Providing childcare at your home.
  • Housekeepers and cleaners – Managing domestic routines and upkeep.
  • Chauffeurs or drivers – Offering regular or ad-hoc transport.
  • Gardeners or estate workers – Maintaining outdoor grounds or land.
  • Private chefs, butlers, or personal assistants – Supporting lifestyle and hospitality needs.

Why is Private Staff Insurance Important?

If you employ domestic staff, you are legally required to carry Employer’s Liability Insurance. Beyond this, Private Staff Cover helps protect against injury claims, disputes, or property damage—giving you peace of mind while maintaining a professional household environment.

Key protections include:

  • Employer’s Liability – Mandatory cover for injuries or illness sustained while working for you.
  • Legal Expenses Cover – Defence against claims of unfair dismissal, discrimination, or contract disputes.
  • Public Liability Protection – If your staff accidentally cause injury or damage during duties.
  • Optional Personal Accident Cover – For serious injury or death of your employee during employment.

What’s Typically Included?

  • Cover for Temporary or Seasonal Staff – Including part-time or ad-hoc workers.

What May Not Be Covered?

  • Employees without a formal written contract.
  • Claims involving illegal workers or non-compliant employment.
  • Injuries outside of working hours or duties.
  • Failure to follow employment law and health & safety regulations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I legally need insurance if I employ a nanny or housekeeper?
Yes. In the UK, if you employ anyone in your home — whether full-time, part-time, or casually — you are legally required to have Employer’s Liability Insurance. This includes nannies, cleaners, gardeners, chefs, and other private staff. Without it, you may face fines of up to £2,500 per day.
What does Private Staff Cover typically include?
Private Staff Insurance typically includes:
  • Employer’s Liability Insurance – Covers injury or illness sustained while working for you.
  • Public Liability Cover – If staff cause accidental damage or harm while on duty.
  • Legal Expenses Cover – Support for disputes, wrongful dismissal claims, or employment tribunals.
  • Optional Personal Accident Cover – For serious injuries to employees in the course of their duties.
Does this insurance cover live-in staff like au pairs or carers?
Yes. Private Staff Cover can be tailored to include live-in employees such as au pairs, carers, or housekeepers. It’s important to disclose their role and living arrangements when setting up the policy so that all risks are properly accounted for.
Will my household staff be covered while working off-site?
In many cases, yes. If your staff accompany you for work-related duties off-site — such as a chauffeur driving to another location or a nanny attending activities with your children — they can be covered under your policy. Always check that your insurer includes off-premises liability as part of the policy wording.
Can I get insurance for temporary or seasonal staff?
Absolutely. Private Staff Cover can be arranged to include short-term, seasonal, or temporary staff, such as event chefs, holiday nannies, or additional housekeeping help. Be sure to inform your broker or insurer about the nature and duration of employment.
How much does Private Staff Insurance cost in the UK?
The cost of Private Staff Insurance varies depending on how many employees you have, their job roles, and your overall lifestyle and risk profile. For most households, it is an affordable addition to a wider High Net Worth insurance policy, often starting from as little as £150 annually when combined with home or estate cover.

At Insurably®, we’re building more than just a brokerage — we’re building trust. Our mission is to make insurance feel more local, more human, and far more efficient. By blending smart technology with good old-fashioned service, we help individuals and businesses get the cover they actually need, without the jargon or corporate nonsense.

We’re part of the Movo Partnership network and backed by industry leaders, but we operate like a startup: agile, ambitious, and focused on doing things properly from day one. Our culture is built around teamwork, doing right by clients, and supporting one another to grow.

This is a chance to join a growing business where your ideas are valued, your experience is respected, and your development is supported every step of the way.

We offer:

  • A friendly, community-first team environment
  • Flexibility and autonomy to grow in your role
  • Support from experienced directors and compliance experts
  • Access to leading systems (Acturis, Zendesk, Aircall)
  • Progression opportunities as we grow
  • Profit-share potential and support with Cert CII studies

What we’re looking for:

  • Someone energised by working in a startup-style environment
  • A confident communicator who enjoys helping clients understand their cover
  • A team player who values collaboration over competition
  • Someone who’s proud to work with integrity and do things properly
  • An individual who sees the value in community-focused insurance

You have:

  • 3+ years of broking experience (ideally commercial lines)
  • A solid understanding of products like Property Owners, Household, or SME Packages
  • Familiarity with Acturis (or able to learn new systems quickly)
  • Strong attention to detail and clear communication skills
  • A collaborative mindset and a genuine interest in helping people feel protected
  • A belief in doing things properly, as part of a team — not working in silos

Even better if:

  • You’ve worked within a growing or startup-style brokerage
  • You’re working towards Cert CII (or would like to — we’ll support you)
  • You’re confident in a client-facing, advice-based role
  • You’re based in or near Northampton and passionate about supporting local businesses

We’d love to hear about how you build relationships, where you’ve added value, and what you’re most proud of. Show us how you work — and we’ll show you how we do things differently at Insurably®.

What is Home Emergency Cover?

Home Emergency Cover provides rapid assistance for unexpected incidents that affect your home's safety or habitability. From boiler breakdowns and burst pipes to electrical failures and pest infestations, it ensures help is on hand when you need it most.

For homeowners, landlords, and families, Home Emergency Cover delivers peace of mind and immediate support when sudden domestic issues arise—day or night.

Who Needs Home Emergency Cover?

If you own or manage a residential property, this type of cover is a smart choice. It’s ideal for:

  • Homeowners – Avoid costly emergency call-outs and repairs.
  • Landlords – Ensure fast response to issues that affect your tenants.
  • First-Time Buyers – Protect your new home from day one.
  • Busy Families – Get 24/7 support for urgent household problems.
  • Remote Workers – Minimise disruption to your home and routine.

Why is Home Emergency Cover Important?

Unexpected home emergencies can be stressful, expensive, and dangerous. This cover gives you access to qualified tradespeople, 24/7 call-outs, and quick resolutions—without worrying about high repair bills.

Common emergencies that Home Emergency Cover protects against include:

  • Boiler Breakdowns – Restore heat and hot water quickly.
  • Burst Pipes or Leaks – Immediate action to prevent flooding and damage.
  • Electrical Failures – Emergency repairs to restore power and safety.
  • Blocked Drains or Toilets – Avoid health hazards and inconvenience.
  • Roof Damage – Urgent repairs for storm or impact damage.

What’s Typically Covered?

Home Emergency Cover usually includes:

  • 24/7 Emergency Helpline – Speak to experts any time of day or night.
  • Call-out Charges – No extra fees for emergency visits.
  • Labour and Parts – Repairs included up to your policy limit.
  • Overnight Accommodation – Costs covered if your home becomes uninhabitable.

What’s Not Covered?

  • General maintenance or pre-existing issues.
  • Boiler servicing or replacements due to age.
  • Non-emergency repairs or cosmetic work.
  • Appliances not connected to your home's main systems.

Other Useful Cover

Depending on your needs, you might also consider:

  • Home Insurance – Broad protection for your building and contents.
  • Boiler Cover – Annual servicing and extended repair options.
  • Landlord Insurance – Extra protection for rental properties.
  • Family Legal Protection – Covers legal costs for common disputes.

Frequently Asked Questions about Home Emergency Cover

What is Home Emergency Cover?
Home Emergency Cover is a type of insurance that provides immediate assistance for unexpected household issues such as boiler breakdowns, burst pipes, electrical failures, and pest infestations. It ensures fast access to qualified tradespeople and emergency support, 24/7.
What does Home Emergency Cover include?
Most Home Emergency policies include:
  • 24/7 emergency helpline access.
  • Call-out fees, labour, and parts for urgent repairs.
  • Boiler and heating system breakdown assistance.
  • Plumbing and drainage emergencies.
  • Electrical failures and loss of power.
  • Emergency roof repairs due to storm damage.
  • Alternative accommodation if the home becomes uninhabitable.
Is Home Emergency Cover worth it?
Yes, for many households it's highly valuable. Emergency repairs can be costly and stressful. Having Home Emergency Cover means you won’t face unexpected bills or delays in getting urgent help when something goes wrong in your home.
Does Home Emergency Cover include boiler breakdowns?
Yes, most Home Emergency policies cover boiler breakdowns. This includes the cost of emergency call-out, labour, and parts (up to the policy limit). Some exclusions may apply, such as age of the boiler or lack of servicing.
Does Home Emergency Cover include plumbing emergencies?
Yes, it typically includes emergency assistance for burst pipes, blocked drains, or leaking water systems. The aim is to prevent further damage and restore safe use of your home quickly.
How much does Home Emergency Cover cost in the UK?
Costs vary depending on the level of cover, your home's age, boiler type, and provider. On average, standalone policies range from £5 to £20 per month. Home Emergency Cover can also be added to your standard home insurance as an optional extra.
How do I make a claim on my Home Emergency Cover?
If you experience a home emergency, call your insurer’s 24/7 helpline immediately. They’ll arrange for a qualified tradesperson to attend. Keep your policy number handy and follow any safety instructions given.

What is High Value Collections Insurance?

High Value Collections Insurance is specialist cover designed to protect rare, valuable, and irreplaceable items such as fine art, antiques, luxury watches, designer jewellery, wine collections, and rare collectibles. Whether held for passion or investment, this tailored insurance ensures your treasured assets are covered properly—at home, in transit, and abroad.

Standard contents policies often fall short when it comes to high-value items. That’s why Insurably® works with leading insurers to provide bespoke, agreed-value cover that reflects the true worth of your collection.

Who Needs High Value Collections Cover?

This specialist protection is essential for individuals and families with valuable possessions that require appraisal, expert handling, or international cover. You may benefit from this insurance if you are:

  • A fine art collector – Paintings, sculptures, or gallery-quality pieces with fluctuating market value.
  • An antiques enthusiast – Furniture, heirlooms, and unique items requiring specialist repair or restoration.
  • A jewellery or watch owner – Items with high single-article value or requiring worldwide protection.
  • A rare wine or spirits investor – Bottles stored at home or in bonded warehouses.
  • A collector of rare books, stamps, or coins – With fluctuating or appreciating market values.

Why is Specialist Cover So Important?

High value items need more than basic contents insurance. Without agreed values, professional valuations, and global protection, you risk being underinsured—or left without cover at all. High Value Collections Insurance offers peace of mind through tailored terms, flexible limits, and access to claims experts who understand how to value, repair, and replace rare items.

Key protections include:

  • Agreed Value Policies – Set and guaranteed item values at the time of insuring, with no haggling during claims.
  • Worldwide All-Risks Cover – Protection at home, in storage, in transit, or on loan to exhibitions.
  • Market Appreciation Protection – Option to include rising asset values over time.
  • Specialist Repairs and Restoration – Access to expert restorers and conservators if needed.

What’s Typically Included?

  • Fine Art Insurance – Tailored to museums, private galleries, or personal collections.
  • Jewellery and Watch Cover – Including wear, storage, and international travel.
  • Antiques Insurance – With flexible valuation terms and specialist repair access.

What May Not Be Covered?

  • Unscheduled or undocumented high-value items.
  • Wear and tear, ageing, or natural deterioration.
  • Damage due to improper storage or lack of maintenance.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is High Net Worth Home Insurance and who is it for?
High Net Worth (HNW) Home Insurance is specialist cover designed for individuals with high-value homes, luxury possessions, and complex lifestyles. It’s ideal for those whose property value, contents, or lifestyle requirements exceed the limits of standard insurance. Typical clients include entrepreneurs, professionals, celebrities, and private clients with heritage homes, bespoke renovations, fine art, or jewellery collections.
What does High Net Worth buildings and contents insurance typically include?
A comprehensive HNW insurance policy typically includes:
  • Unlimited or high-limit rebuild cover for unique or listed homes.
  • Worldwide all-risks cover for valuables such as jewellery, watches, and designer items.
  • Fine art and antique insurance with agreed value settlements.
  • High-value contents cover including smart tech, home cinemas, and bespoke furniture.
  • Personal and employer’s liability, including cover for domestic staff.
  • Alternative accommodation that matches your current standard of living.
Do I need a specialist insurer for my high-value home?
Yes. If your home is worth over £750,000, or your contents exceed £100,000 in value, a standard insurer may not offer adequate protection. Specialist HNW insurers understand the complexities of luxury properties, offer broader cover, and provide personalised underwriting that reflects your lifestyle and assets.
Can I insure my jewellery, watches, and art collections under my home policy?
Yes — High Net Worth policies typically offer worldwide all-risks cover for high-value items such as jewellery, luxury watches, fine art, antiques, and designer clothing. Items can be covered on an agreed value basis, ensuring that in the event of a loss, you receive the full insured amount without depreciation or disputes.
What is agreed value insurance and why is it important?
Agreed value insurance means that you and your insurer agree upfront on the value of an item or property. If it's lost or damaged beyond repair, you'll receive the full amount agreed — no haggling, no depreciation. This is especially important for art, jewellery, rare items, and classic cars whose market values may fluctuate or be difficult to replace.
What extra cover can I add to a High Net Worth home policy?
Optional extras for HNW clients include:
  • Cyber insurance – Protection from cybercrime, identity theft, and ransomware.
  • Travel insurance – Annual cover for worldwide trips with cancellation and medical protection.
  • Second home cover – Including overseas properties or UK holiday homes.
  • Household staff cover – Liability and employment protection for cleaners, chefs, or nannies.
  • Private collections insurance – For wine, watches, fashion, and memorabilia.

What is Personal Possessions Cover?

Personal Possessions Cover is an optional insurance add-on that protects your valuable belongings when you're away from home. Whether it’s a mobile phone, laptop, bicycle, jewellery, or handbag, this cover ensures you're protected against accidental loss, damage, or theft—anywhere in the UK and sometimes even abroad.

For commuters, students, professionals, and families on the go, Personal Possessions Insurance provides peace of mind that your everyday essentials are protected wherever life takes you.

Who Needs Personal Possessions Insurance?

If you regularly carry valuable items outside your home, this cover is essential. It’s ideal for:

  • Mobile phone users – Protection against loss or damage on the move.
  • Cyclists and commuters – Cover for bikes, headphones, and gadgets while travelling.
  • Jewellery wearers – Insurance for rings, watches, and accessories worn daily.
  • Students or remote workers – Cover for laptops, tablets, and tech used in cafés, libraries, or while travelling.

Why is Personal Possessions Cover Important?

Standard contents insurance usually only protects your belongings while they’re inside your home. Without Personal Possessions Cover, you're likely not insured for loss or damage when you're out and about. This cover bridges that gap—giving you the freedom to take your items with you without constant worry.

Typical situations Personal Possessions Insurance covers include:

  • Lost phones or gadgets – Left behind on the train or dropped while out and about.
  • Stolen handbags or backpacks – Taken while shopping or travelling.
  • Damaged electronics – Cracked phone screens or water-damaged tablets.
  • Theft of jewellery or watches – While worn in public or left in a locked locker.
  • Bike thefts away from home – Even when chained or locked up.

What’s Covered with Personal Possessions Insurance?

This cover typically includes protection for:

  • Accidental loss or damage – For items dropped, broken, or misplaced.
  • Theft while away from home – Including in public places or locked vehicles.
  • Cover in the UK and abroad – Often includes up to 60 days of worldwide cover.
  • Named high-value items – Like watches, engagement rings, or designer bags.
  • Everyday essentials – Like phones, laptops, tablets, headphones, and glasses.

What’s Not Covered?

  • Wear and tear or gradual deterioration of items.
  • Negligence (e.g. leaving items unattended in plain sight).
  • Unspecified high-value items not declared individually.
  • Items used for business purposes unless specifically listed.

Additional Cover You May Need

Personal Possessions Cover works best when combined with other protections. You may also want to consider:

  • Contents Insurance – Protects items inside your home.
  • Accidental Damage Cover – Extra protection for one-off mishaps inside your home.
  • Home Emergency Cover – Deals with urgent issues like burst pipes or boiler failures.
  • Family Legal Protection – Covers legal costs from home or consumer disputes.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Personal Possessions Cover?
Personal Possessions Cover is an optional add-on to your contents insurance that protects valuable items you regularly take outside your home—such as mobile phones, laptops, jewellery, watches, or bicycles—against loss, theft, or accidental damage anywhere in the UK and sometimes abroad.
What does Personal Possessions Insurance cover?
It covers your personal belongings when you take them outside your home. Commonly covered items include:
  • Mobile phones and tablets
  • Laptops, cameras, and headphones
  • Jewellery and watches
  • Bicycles and sports equipment
  • Handbags, wallets, and designer accessories
This cover typically includes theft, accidental loss, and accidental damage—whether you're out shopping, on holiday, or commuting.
Do I need Personal Possessions Cover if I already have contents insurance?
Yes—standard contents insurance typically only covers items while they’re inside your home. If you want protection for valuables when you’re out and about, Personal Possessions Cover is essential.
Is accidental loss of my phone or jewellery covered outside the home?
If you’ve added Personal Possessions Cover to your policy, then yes—accidental loss or theft of personal items like phones, jewellery, or handbags outside the home is usually covered, up to your chosen policy limits.
Am I covered when I travel abroad?
Many policies include worldwide cover for up to 60 days a year—but this can vary by insurer. Be sure to check your policy details if you're planning to travel with expensive items.

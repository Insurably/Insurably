What is Professional Indemnity Insurance?

Professional Indemnity insurance protects you against claims made by clients or other third parties for losses or damages caused by your professional services or advice. This insurance covers legal costs, compensation payments, and helps safeguard your professional reputation in case of alleged negligence, errors, or omissions.

Why do I need Professional Indemnity insurance?

In today's litigious environment, a small mistake can lead to significant financial consequences. Professional Indemnity insurance provides crucial protection, whether it's a mistake in advice, a document error, or a breach of professional duty. This coverage allows you to conduct your business with confidence and peace of mind.

Whether you're a consultant, freelancer, or business owner, Professional Indemnity insurance offers you the protection you need to focus on what you do best, without the fear of unexpected legal challenges.
